Boiled Egg

Description

Boil an egg!

Ingredients

  • An egg
  • Water to boil

Steps

  1. Fill a pan with cold water.
  2. Put the egg in the pan.
  3. Turn on the heat and bring the water to the boil
  4. Leave the egg in the boiling water for 5-6 minutes for soft boiled or 10-12 minutes for hard boiled.
